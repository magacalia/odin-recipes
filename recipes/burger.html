American Burger

Description

An American burger is a classic meal featuring a juicy beef patty served in a soft bun with toppings like cheese, lettuce, tomato, and pickles. Known for its satisfying balance of flavors and textures, itâ€™s a favorite comfort food worldwide.

To prepare, season the beef, form patties, cook until perfectly browned, and assemble with fresh toppings and condiments. Serve warm for a deliciously hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g of ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp of black pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • Pickle slices
  • 1 small onion, sliced (optional)
  • Condiments of choice (e.g., ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground beef with salt and black pepper. Divide into 4 equal portions and form into round patties, about 2 cm thick.
  2. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they reach your preferred doneness. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  3. Lightly toast the burger buns on the skillet or grill for extra flavor.
  4. Assemble the burger by layering lettuce, the beef patty with melted cheese, tomato slices, pickles, and onion on the bottom half of the bun. Add condiments to taste.
  5. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
